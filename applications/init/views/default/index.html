Proven Process

1) Free Consultation

Schedule our free, honest and professional analysis of your practice, medicaid lives and EMR to see if PCMH training is right for you. There are no fees or strings attached for our advice!

Schedule a Meeting

2) Gap Analysis & Training

Once you agree to our pricing and services, we will immediately send one of our trainers to analyze and coordinate with your practice to begin your transformation into a medical home.

3) Data Gathering

One of our PCMH application managers will be assigned to your practice to gather data from your EMR, and handle the complex documentation needed for PCMH certification.

4) Submission & Certification

One of our PCMH Certified Content Experts (CCE) will review and submit your application to NCQA. After obtaining Level 2 or 3 certification, your HMOs will incentivise your practice monthly or quarterly.
