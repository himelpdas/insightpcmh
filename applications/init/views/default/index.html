With over one hundred Level 3 practices, Insight Management® is one of New York City's leading experts in NCQA's Patient Centered Medical Home (PCMH) certificaition.

Proven Process

1) Free Consultation

Schedule our free, honest and professional analysis of your practice, medicaid lives and EMR to see if PCMH training is right for you. There are no fees or strings attached for our advice!

Schedule a meeting

2) Gap Analysis & Training

Once you agree to our pricing, service and guarantee, we will send out one of our trainers to coordinate with and analyze your practice to begin your transformation into a medical home.

3) Data Gathering

One of our PCMH application managers will be assigned to your practice to remotely gather data from your EMR, and handle all of the complex documentation needed for PCMH certification.

4) Submission & Certification

One of our PCMH Certified Content Experts (CCE) will carefully review and submit your application to NCQA. You will get your results within 45 - 60 days!
